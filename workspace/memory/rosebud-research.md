# Rosebud User Research — Knowledge Base

Last updated: 2026-03-23

## What Rosebud Is
AI journaling app. Users write entries, AI responds with reflections and questions ("Dig Deeper"). Core features: memory across sessions, adaptive personas (Ally/Mirror/Coach), voice input. Priced at $12.99/mo (Bloom), with Thrive tier in development.

## Primary Segment Decision: Therapy-Fluent
Chosen as primary target. ~45M U.S. TAM. Highest WTP (already pay $150-400/mo for therapy). Therapist referrals convert at 73%. Lowest churn (19-27%). Competitive window: 12-18 months before Dawn and others fill the space.

Positioning: "The other 167 hours" — between therapy sessions.

## The Core Research Finding
**Behavioral metrics on day one are identical across core, dormant, and churned users.** Same entry counts, same engagement intensity. Writing *orientation* separates them.

Three qualities predict retention, visible from day one:
1. **Process orientation** over relief orientation — came to understand, not to be fixed
2. **Existing relationship with introspection** — any form, any depth
3. **Observer-self capacity** — can watch themselves feel rather than being consumed by feeling

Life severity does NOT differentiate. A process-oriented user in acute crisis retains as well as one in stability.

## ICP Qualifying Filter
People who can feel pain and narrate it at the same time:
- Meta-cognitive capacity under distress
- Exhausted support networks
- Compound stressors (3-5 simultaneous)
- Friction tolerance — willing to stay in hard conversations
- Processing orientation — messy, contradictory, real entries
- Treats AI as a relationship — pushes back, redirects, says goodnight

## Jobs to Be Done (ranked)
1. Help me process difficult emotions when I don't have enough support
2. Help me see patterns and insights I can't see alone
3. Help me extend and deepen therapeutic work I'm already doing
4. Give me a journaling practice I can stick with
5. Help me navigate a major life transition

## Activation Triggers
- 7+ entries during trial → 85%+ conversion; 15+ entries → 92.5%
- Daily cadence more predictive than total volume
- Friend/family referrals convert at 79%, therapist at 73%, TikTok at 39%
- "Unlock insights" as goal converts at 70% vs 64% for "spark creativity"
- Entry depth signals product fit — non-converters write shorter at every stage
- Memory is #1 differentiator vs ChatGPT (59% of active users)

## Churn Drivers
1. **Repetitive/shallow AI responses** — #1 product churn driver (44% active, 70% churned)
2. **Months 2-4 critical window** — novelty wears off, gradual decline
3. **Memory failures** — 29-44% cite as improvement area; "betrayal of trust"
4. **Price** — real but secondary; masks two profiles (can't afford vs. won't pay for shallow)
5. **Life circumstances** — 33-44% cite busyness, not grievances; 68% say they'd return
6. **Tone/safety** — "patronizing," harmful responses to abuse content
7. **ChatGPT** — main competitor threat, beatable on memory + journaling interface

## Segmentation Lenses

### Lens A: Identity-Based
- 🧡 Therapy Goers (~26% of users, 50-55% of retained ideal users)
- 🔥 Life Rebuilders (~80-85% of new ideal users arrive in crisis)
- 🪨 Chronic Strugglers (~10-15%, Sporadic Tenured cluster, highly profitable)
- 🧩 Neurodivergent Navigators (~4% explicit, likely 15-20%)
- 🌱 Growth Curious (~17%, Late Bloomers convert at 78%)
- 🩺 Therapists & Coaches (<3% as users, huge as distribution channel)
- ✍️ Personal Writers (~5-10%)

### Lens B: Temporal/Lifecycle
- ⚡ In Crisis — 80-85% of ideal users enter here
- 🔄 In Process — economic core, 31% of users
- 🛡️ In Maintenance — most profitable per user, hardest to acquire

### Lens C: Orientation-Based (strongest retention predictor)
- 🔬 Process-Oriented — 55-65% of core retained
- 🆘 Relief-Oriented — 50-60% of churned (observer-self is LEARNABLE)
- 📋 Structure-Seeking — highest-ROI conversion target, 20-25% of users

### Lens D: Interactional Mode (most programmatically detectable)
- 🔬 Collaborative Processor — thinking partner, most robust profile
- 🆘 Cathartic Releaser — pressure release, prompt deflection
- 🛠️ Directive Operator — instrumental use, not captured by Lens C
- 📖 Life Archiver — daily narration, wants witness not mirror

## First Day Analysis (186 high-activation retained users)
- ~85% arrived at acute inflection point
- Typical user carrying 3-5 compounding stressors
- Near-total absence of performative journaling
- Day one volume up to 18,000 words ("uncorking")
- Many intense entries written late at night mid-spiral
- Temporal availability during crisis = deepest moat
- Relationship dissolution accounts for 45-70% of first-day entries

## Comparative Analysis (1,132 day-one entries: 273 core, 256 dormant, 603 churned)
- Core finding: "self-as-project" vs "situation-as-crisis"
- 60-75% of core entries show pre-existing introspective work
- 50-60% of churned entries read as acute crisis reaching for relief
- Dormant split almost evenly between orientations
- ~15-25% of churned users indistinguishable from process-oriented on day one
- Observer-self capacity confirmed learnable across all 4 analysis runs

## Life Triggers (why people show up)
1. Mental health crisis/escalation
2. Relationship rupture or strain
3. Therapy gap (between sessions, can't afford more, waitlisted)
4. Health crisis / chronic illness
5. Job loss / career disruption
6. Feeling out of control / overwhelmed

## Who to Serve Wrapped (2026-03-24) — Additional Insights
- 11,000 users analyzed (Aug-Oct cohort) → 442 (4%) remain core subscribers
- 15% of trialists "came in hot" (7/7 trial days, 2,000+ words) — convert + retain at much higher rates
- But 35% of hot trialists still churned within 4 months — behavior alone doesn't predict retention
- 90% of hot trialists arrive with something weighing on them — but the crisis has almost no correlation with whether someone stays
- How someone engages predicts LTV 7-8x better than why they showed up
- "Rosebud Fit" = Motivation × Readiness
- Relief motivation: high spike, fades as crisis passes
- Growth motivation: builds and compounds over time (mastery loop)
- Readiness = metacognition + self-honesty + agency (maps to "psychological flexibility" in clinical psych)
- Users who score high across all 6 ICP traits churn at 39% vs 57% and pay ~30% more
- More roles the user uses Rosebud in (ally + mirror + coach), the more likely to retain
- AI as partner = 2nd strongest predictor of trial activation
